# ZIGChain Summit 2026 — Reddit Outreach Dashboard: Design Ideas

## Idea 1 — "Intelligence Terminal"
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement**: Brutalist Data Terminal meets Institutional Finance
**Core Principles**: Raw information density, monospace precision, high-contrast hierarchy, zero decoration
**Color Philosophy**: Near-black backgrounds (#0A0C0F), electric green (#00FF88) for active/live data, amber (#F5A623) for warnings, white for primary text. Evokes Bloomberg terminal authority.
**Layout Paradigm**: Full-width horizontal bands — a fixed top command bar, a scrollable data grid below, with a right-side panel for expanded content. No cards, just rows and columns.
**Signature Elements**: Monospace typography for data, blinking cursor indicators, ASCII-style dividers
**Interaction Philosophy**: Click a row to expand inline — no modals, no navigation. Everything visible at once.
**Animation**: Subtle scan-line effect on load, data rows fade-in sequentially like a terminal populating
**Typography System**: JetBrains Mono for all data, Space Grotesk for headings
</text>

## Idea 2 — "Onchain Intelligence Brief" (SELECTED)
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement**: Editorial Intelligence Report — inspired by financial research desks and premium newsletters
**Core Principles**: Information-first hierarchy, editorial whitespace, restrained color, typographic authority
**Color Philosophy**: Deep navy (#0F1629) as the primary background, warm off-white (#F7F4EE) for content areas, ZIGChain teal/green (#00D4AA) as the single accent, charcoal (#1C2333) for secondary surfaces. Conveys institutional credibility without coldness.
**Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric two-column layout — a narrow left sidebar for navigation/filters, a wide right content area for the main data. Top hero banner with summit details. Avoids centered grid monotony.
**Signature Elements**: Thin horizontal rule dividers, numbered list items with large ordinal typography, subtle gradient overlays on cards
**Interaction Philosophy**: Filter by topic/subreddit category, expand replies inline with smooth accordion, copy-to-clipboard for reply text
**Animation**: Cards slide in from left on filter change, reply text fades in on expand, subtle hover lift on cards
**Typography System**: Playfair Display for headings (editorial authority), DM Sans for body (readable, modern)
</text>

## Idea 3 — "Signal Map"
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement**: Data Visualization Dashboard — inspired by intelligence monitoring platforms
**Core Principles**: Visual data-first, color-coded signals, spatial organization, real-time feel
**Color Philosophy**: Dark charcoal (#111827) base, with a vibrant signal palette — green for high-relevance, amber for medium, blue for informational. ZIGChain's brand teal as the primary accent.
**Layout Paradigm**: Top summary metrics bar, then a filterable card grid (3-column on desktop) with each card representing one Reddit conversation. A sticky sidebar shows the summit info panel.
**Signature Elements**: Relevance score badges, subreddit tags as colored pills, "copy reply" floating action
**Interaction Philosophy**: Filter by topic category, sort by age/relevance, expand any card to see full reply suggestion
**Animation**: Card grid reflows with spring animation on filter, score badges pulse on hover
**Typography System**: Syne for display headings (geometric, distinctive), Source Sans Pro for body
</text>

## Selected Design: "Onchain Intelligence Brief" (Idea 2)

The editorial intelligence report style best serves this use case — it presents research findings with the authority of a professional brief, while remaining approachable and action-oriented. The asymmetric layout avoids generic grid monotony, and the copy-to-clipboard interaction makes the reply suggestions immediately actionable.
